Hamilton Chess Club - Our Journey

Our Story

Hamilton Chess Club was founded in September 2025 by Balaji Narayanan and Deepak Gupta with a mission to provide structured chess coaching for kids and students across Menomonee Falls, Pewaukee, Sussex, Waukesha, Franklin, Milwaukee, Brookfield, and surrounding Wisconsin communities.

What began as a growing interest among students in local schools quickly revealed a larger gap—many young players wanted to learn chess seriously and compete in tournaments, but lacked access to consistent chess coaching, structured curriculum, and organized competitive training programs in their area.

To address this need, Hamilton Chess Club was created as a local chess training academy and community-based chess club, offering professional guidance, beginner-to-advanced instruction, and a supportive learning environment where students can build strong chess fundamentals and improve steadily over time.

Our coaching approach focuses on both skill development and competitive readiness, helping students participate confidently in USCF-rated and WSCF-rated chess tournaments while building long-term improvement through practice, strategy, and mentorship.

Beyond competitive results, our mission is to develop essential life skills through chess, including critical thinking, discipline, patience, focus, problem-solving, confidence, and resilience.

Proudly built as a Parent–Student Volunteering Initiative, Hamilton Chess Club has grown into a trusted local chess community serving families across Southeast Wisconsin. We continue to expand access to quality chess education in areas where structured coaching is limited, helping students grow both on and off the chessboard.

Professional Chess Training

Meet Our Coaches

Our club is led by a Certified & Experienced Top-Rated FIDE Tournament Professional Coach who mentors serious players through structured training, FIDE title preparation, and professional tournament coaching.

Designed for young talents, competitive players, and dedicated adult learners, our program combines expert instruction, personalized guidance, and modern training tools to help students achieve competitive success and long-term chess excellence.

Lead Coach

Coach Edgar Talayko

Top-Rated FIDE Tournament Professional Coach

Edgar Talayko is a professional chess player and coach with over 10 years of playing and coaching experience and a peak rating of 2039.

In addition to founding and managing numerous tournaments, clubs,and chess groups throughout the Milwaukee area, Edgar has taught at CheckMatesUSA, and the International Academy of Chess since 2016. We are proud to have him join our Hamilton Chess Club since September 2025 and share his coaching philosophy to our young achievers.

His coaching philosophy focuses on personalized instruction tailored to each student's goals, strengths, and learning style. Through structured lessons, game analysis, opening preparation, tactical training, and tournament coaching, he helps players improve both their chess skills and strategic thinking.

From complete beginners to competitive tournament players, Edgar provides the expertise and guidance needed to achieve measurable improvement and lasting success.

Year-Round Chess Programs

What We Offer

We offer in-person chess programs for students throughout the Menomonee Falls Area during Spring, Summer, and Fall sessions. Our programs are designed for Beginners, Intermediate, and Advanced players ages 5+, providing a structured pathway from learning the fundamentals to competing successfully in rated tournaments.

♙

Beginner Training

Learn the fundamentals of chess including piece movement, checkmate patterns, opening principles, tactics, and basic strategy in a fun and engaging environment.

♘

Intermediate Training

Develop stronger tactical vision, positional understanding, strategic planning, and tournament readiness while building confidence in competitive play.

♛

Advanced & Competitive Program

Build a solid foundation in advanced tactics, positional play, opening preparation, and tournament strategy. Designed for ambitious players pursuing higher ratings, FIDE recognition, and consistent tournament success.


Students learn deep calculation techniques, advanced strategic planning, endgame mastery, and preparation against stronger opposition while refining intuition and decision-making skills.
