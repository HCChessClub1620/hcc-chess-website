About our Chess Journey

Hamilton Chargers Chess Club was founded by Balaji Narayanan and Deepak Gupta in September 2025. The idea began when we noticed a strong enthusiasm among students in our school district who were eagerly waiting for a structured chess program.

This motivation inspired us to create a space where young minds could explore chess deeply, build thinking skills, and grow through structured coaching and practice.

Our mission is to develop discipline, patience, strategy, and confidence through chess, while building a strong connection with every student we mentor.

This academy is proudly run as a Parent–Student Volunteering Initiative. We are grateful for the active participation of families and students. We have successfully hosted multiple local tournaments including WSCF and USCF events.

What We Offer

We offer In-person chess programs for kids in the Menomonee Falls Area for kids throughout the year, including Spring, Fall and Summer programs for Beginners, Intermediate and Advanced Level Students starting age 5+ years.

Beginner Foundation level chess training focusing on basics.
Intermediate Skill-building programs to improve tactics and strategy.
Advanced Competitive training for tournament-level players.
