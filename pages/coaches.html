Meet Our Coaches

Our club is led by a Certified & Experienced Top-Rated FIDE Tournament Professional Coach who mentors serious players through structured training, FIDE title preparation, and professional tournament coaching.

Designed for young talents, competitive players, and dedicated adult learners, our program combines expert instruction, personalized guidance, and modern training tools to help students achieve competitive success and long-term chess excellence.

Lead Coach

Coach Edgar Talayko

Top-Rated FIDE Tournament Professional Coach

Edgar Talayko is a professional chess player and coach with over 10 years of playing and coaching experience and a peak rating of 2039.

In addition to founding and managing numerous tournaments, clubs,and chess groups throughout the Milwaukee area, Edgar has taught at CheckMatesUSA, and the International Academy of Chess since 2016. We are proud to have him join our Hamilton Chess Club since September 2025 and share his coaching philosophy to our young achievers.

His coaching philosophy focuses on personalized instruction tailored to each student's goals, strengths, and learning style. Through structured lessons, game analysis, opening preparation, tactical training, and tournament coaching, he helps players improve both their chess skills and strategic thinking.

From complete beginners to competitive tournament players, Edgar provides the expertise and guidance needed to achieve measurable improvement and lasting success.
